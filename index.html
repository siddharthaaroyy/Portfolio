VALIDATION & RESULTS

Zero lookahead. Ever.

Most stock-prediction papers shuffle their data into a single random train/test split — and quietly let the future leak into training. This model is evaluated with an expanding-window walk-forward protocol: the training window only ever grows forward in time, and validation always sits just ahead of it, never behind.
